Community Work

Your support is crucial for our community projects. Please consider donating to help us make a difference in the lives of those in need.

Food Pantry

Our food pantry provides essential food supplies to families in need, ensuring that no one in our community goes hungry.

Clothing Drive

The clothing drive collects gently used clothes to distribute to individuals and families who cannot afford them.

Community Garden

This project encourages sustainable gardening practices while providing fresh produce for those in need.

Youth Mentoring Program

We pair at-risk youth with caring adults to provide guidance, support, and encouragement for a brighter future.

Senior Outreach

This initiative focuses on visiting and supporting elderly members of our community, offering companionship and assistance.

Homeless Shelter Support

We provide assistance to local homeless shelters through donations and volunteer work to help those experiencing homelessness.

Health Clinics

Our church collaborates with local health providers to offer free health clinics, ensuring everyone has access to healthcare.

Community Clean-Up Days

We organize regular clean-up events to beautify our neighborhoods and promote environmental stewardship.

After-School Programs

These programs provide safe and educational environments for children after school, promoting learning and social skills.

Disaster Relief Fund

We raise funds and collect supplies to assist those affected by natural disasters in our community and beyond.
